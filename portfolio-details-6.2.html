Lily of the Valley

I composed Lily of the Valley near the end of 2022, as a special piece for my wife's entrance at our wedding. The song reflects my deep feelings for her and the excitement of that moment. Inspired by her beauty, I used the piano to create a lovely melody that captures our journey and lifelong commitment to each other. The song is a symbol of our love story, a melody that will always remind us of our shared moments.
