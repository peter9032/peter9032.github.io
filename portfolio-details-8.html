Stuffed Salmon on Pea Curry

Ingredients
  • 1 fillet pre-stuffed salmon
  • 1 lb frozen peas
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1 tsp tumeric
  • 4 leaves fresh basil
  • 1 tsp cayenne pepper
  • Freshly ground salt & pepper
  • 2 tsp garlic powder
  • 1/2 cup chicken stock
  • 1 tbsp olive oil

    • Instructions (30 min)

      1. Prep Ingredients
      • Make sure all of the ingredients are within reach
      • Place two skillets on the stove. The Salmon pan on high and the pea curry pan on medium-high
      2. Start the Pea Curry
      • Place the frozen peas into the skillet and cook until the peas are warm
      • Pour the stock and cream over the peas and continue to simmer. The goal is to cook off the liquids while creating a soft and creamy curry
      • As the mixture thickens, add the tumeric, cayenne, and garlic powder
      • Season as desired with fresh salt and pepper
      • Crush the basil leaves a little and stir into the curry. You will remove these leaves later so don't break them up
      • Once the mixture is in a good spot but is still runny start the salmon
      3. Pan Fry the Salmon
      • Place the olive oil into the hot pan. It should begin to smoke a little
      • Lay the fillet into the pan away from your body so the hot oil doesn't splash on you
      • 75% of the cook time will be on the first side. Make sure it is golden and crispy before flipping to the other side
      • It is totally fine to leave the salmon medium rare in the middle but cook to your desired doneness
      4. Plate and Eat!
      • Remove the basil leaves from the curry
      • Place a generous portion of the curry onto a plate
      • Place the crispiest side of salmon up on top of the curry in the middle of the place
      • Enjoy!
