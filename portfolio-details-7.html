French Toast with Apple Rum Butter Sauce

Ingredients
  • 2 fresh apples
  • 4 oz spiced rum
  • 4 tbsp butter
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 1 cup cane sugar
  • 1 tbsp milk
  • 2 eggs
  • 4 slices bread

    • Instructions (30 min)

      1. Prep Ingredients
      • Wash then cut each apple into eight slices and the chop those slices into 1/4 inch pieces making sure to leave the skin intact (keeps apples from getting mushy)
      • In a container that fits the bread slices, beat the eggs, milk, and half of the cinnamon until well blended
      • Place two skillets on the stove. The French toast pan on medium and the rum suace pan on medium-high
      2. Start the Rum Sauce
      • Place the sugar in the rum suace pan and carefully watch as the sugar melts. If the pan is too hot the sugar will burn. Ideally, the sugar should slowly melt and turn brown but if it gets too dark lower the heat
      • Once the sugar is fully melted pour in the apple pieces and evenly distribute. It will take a few minutes for the apples to heat up. Do not stir!
      • The apples will begin to heat and start losing liquid which will help mix the apples with the melted sugar. It is okay if some of the sugar hardens. The goal is to create a smooth carmel like liquid when finished.
      • When the apples are well mixed with the melted sugar, add the remaining cinnamon and stir well. Place half the butter in the mixture and let it melt. Stir until this is well incorporated.
      • After a few more minutes of allowing the ingredients to blend, pour in the spiced sum and stir. It will take 5-10 minutes for the alcohol to cook out. During this time you can make the French toast
      3. Make the French Toast
      • Melt the remaining butter in the French toast pan
      • Dip the slices of bread into the egg mixture and place on the hot pan
      • Cook on boths sides until each is no longer soggy and a deep golden brown
      4. Plate and Eat!
      • Place the French toast on a plate and drizzle the warm rum butter suace all over the top
      • Eat it while its hot!
